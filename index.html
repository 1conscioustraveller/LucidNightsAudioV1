About Lucid Nights

Born in a 24-hour café somewhere between Berlin and the internet, Lucid Nights Audio is a micro-studio obsessed with one thing: tools that feel like night-time. No investors, no bloat, no light mode. Just pure, focused utilities that load instantly, respect your CPU, and look dangerous on stage.

Manifesto

  • Dark interfaces are not a theme—they are the default.
  • Every kilobyte must justify its existence.
  • Accessibility includes being usable at 128 bpm under strobe lights.
  • Open-source when possible, transparent when not.
